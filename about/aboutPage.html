Gabriel Lamanna


From Pittsburgh, Pennyslvania, Gabriel works as a Mapping and Localization Engineer at self-driving car company Aurora. In 2014 he attended Slippery Rock University of Pennsylvania studying physics and Environmental Science. In 2016, Gabriel transferred to the Univeristy of Pittsburgh where he studied computer and information science. Gabriel took up an internship at Aviation and Rail giant Bombardier where he was a software test engineer working with regional automated train control software on the generic product team. Gabriel graduated in fall of 2018 with a degree in Information science. Upon graduating, Gabriel took up data analyst role at BossaNova Robotics before landing a job at the self-driving startup.
